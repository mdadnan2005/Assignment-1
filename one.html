My Travel Vlog

My name is Md Adnan Abdullah. Now I am a student of Honors 1st year of Noakhali Science and Technology University. Once upon a time, I was an admision candidate. I went to Sylhet for an admission exam in Sylhet Engineering College.Then I made a tour to the famous places of Sylhet like Jaflong, Sada pthore, malnichora Cha Bagan, Sylhet International Cricket Stadium. It was really an amazing tour with my friend Shaon.

We staerted our journey from Mirpir,Dhaka. At 5AM, we became ready and out for the train which was at 6.30AM from Airport Railway Station. We took a CNG and gone to the station. Enjoying the view of the view of the way to we reached to Sylhet Railway Station. It was really enjoying when we was passing Sreemangal, Tea Garden, hills. Reaching there, we went to a hotel to eat and then we took rest in a mosque.
